Customisation

Keep it simple: your club identity, done cleanly and consistently across your kit.

What you can customise

  • Rashies: logo on chest, plus name & number on the back.
  • Vests: logo + number (front and/or back depending on your design).
  • Balaclavas: colour options (and potential logo placement depending on design).
Tip: Send your logo as a PNG/SVG and your roster list (names + numbers). We’ll align the layout so every piece matches.

How ordering works

  • Tell us what items you need (balaclavas / rashies / vests) and quantities.
  • Share colours, sizes, and your customisation details.
  • We confirm a digital mock-up for approval.
  • Production starts after approval — we’ll send timelines with your quote.
